Staying Grounded: A Rusty Gate Estate Wedding

Earthy Tones, Minimalist Modern Fair

Click to save to favorite
Click to save to favorite

Alex had recently moved from Albury to Geelong making planning a wedding tougher than usual. Working as a child psychologist with a super busy schedule she met up with her wedding planner from Darling Don't Panic, to ease the wedding preparation process by setting monthly tasks. Months of planning for Alex and Justin turned out to become a gorgeous earthy toned wedding with modern and minimalist flair. Set amongst the Bellarine Peninsula, in an award winning Australian olive grove, this young couple’s venue at the Rusty Gate Estate featured expansive views, and grand gardens. The reception’s ceiling floral installation was one of the most important features for Alex. So after meeting with a few florists, Charlie & Co really understood the unstructured and organic look Alex was going for. This resulted in hearty floral bouquets and centerpieces that gave the minimal place setting and reception decor a grounded and comforting design. With Truffleduck’s thrilling meal experience catering the reception, this wedding was rich with not only picturesque design, but delicious ambiance.
