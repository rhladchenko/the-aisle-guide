About veil tales weddings

You have just said "YES" to the most important person on the planet and now it's time to plan your celebration! Veil Tales Weddings is a premier destination wedding and event planning service in California and Las Vegas.

Our goal and our passion is to turn your vision into a celebration as unique and unforgettable as your love story. We are here to infuse joy into your planning process - staying with you from the time you say yes until after the " I Do's."

Your job is to take a breath and enjoy each moment of your day surrounded by family and friends.

Maybe you're dreaming of a rustic vineyard wedding or an intimate gathering on the beach. Whatever you're dreaming of, Veil Tales Weddings is here to help create that perfect day exactly the way you want it!
